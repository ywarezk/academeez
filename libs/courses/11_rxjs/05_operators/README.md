Operators are function that gives us the ability to create new observables or manipulate our observables.

They give us an easy, declarative and reusable way for handling our async problems.

In this chapter we will cover
what are operators
what are the operators that rxjs supply us
how we can use operators to handle errors
how we can construct our own custom operators
We will go over the popular most used operators.

And learn to really master rxjs by understanding operator thoroughly
